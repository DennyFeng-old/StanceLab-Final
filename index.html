About Us

Established in 2010, Stance Lab is the Bay Area’s Premier European shop. From maintenance, repairs, performance and wheel upgrades to window tinting, vehicle vinyl wrapping, and paint protection, we have the knowledge and experience to keep your vehicle running great while looking at its best.

Thus, with a combined 35 years in automotive industry, we have seen a lot of wrong doing's. Our motivation to start a European specialist shop is to provide our customer's with honest and first rate services. We use factory certified diagnostic tools as well as OEM parts to keep your vehicle running like it just rolled off the lot.

Feel Free to lounge in our waiting area with complimentary Wi-FI and Starbucks coffee or take advantage of our shuttle services to San Bruno Bart and Caltrain stations.

Services

Repair

Rest assured your vehicle will be properly diagnosed and repaired using our factory authorized computers, Snap-On scan tools, and ASE and dealership Master certified technicians.

Maintenance

Using OEM quality parts allows us to properly perform the required maintenance to keep your vehicle running reliably for years to come.

Performance

Whether it be bolt-ons, tuning upgrades, or complete engine rebuilds, our team of knowledgeable staff can help you achieve the maximum potential from your vehicle.

Wheels & Tires

Only choosing industry leading companies in quality workmanship and warranty standards, we can provide you with the best possible wheels and tires to enhance the look and feel of your vehicle.

Paint Protection Film

Protect your investment from rock chips and road debris with Suntek Ultra paint protection film. Partial, full front end, or entire car, we can help keep your vehicle looking as good as new.

Vinyl Wrap

Tired of your vehicle’s color but don't want to paint it? Perform a color change wrap or just wrap some accent pieces for a whole new look. It is completely reversible and will not damage your original paint.

Charity Work

It was an honor to partner with Happy Hollow Park and Zoo to create these wonderful print wraps. These vehicles are used to transport their awesome animal helpers to visit schools and other events for educational purposes. So glad we could be a part of this project!
